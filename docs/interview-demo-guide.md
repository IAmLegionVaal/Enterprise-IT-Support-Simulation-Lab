# Interview Demonstration Guide

## Five-minute demonstration

1. Explain the simulated organization and support scope.
2. Run the case generator and select a scenario.
3. Open the generated case brief and describe your initial questions.
4. Explain how the evidence checklist prevents guesswork.
5. Walk through the escalation summary and customer communication template.

## Suggested scenario

Use `SUP-006` — repeated account lockout.

Discuss how you would:

- Confirm the timeline and user impact
- Identify the originating device or service
- Check saved credentials, mapped resources, mobile clients, and scheduled tasks
- Preserve relevant authentication evidence
- Avoid repeatedly unlocking the account without finding the source
- Escalate when the source is a server-side service or domain issue

## What interviewers should notice

- Troubleshooting is hypothesis-driven
- Evidence is recorded before changes are made
- Escalation contains actionable technical detail
- Communication is separated into technical and customer-facing language
- Preventive actions are included after resolution
